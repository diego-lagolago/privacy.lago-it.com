Privacy Policy — RewardU

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Overview

RewardU is a habit tracking and reward management app for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how RewardU handles your data.

Data Collection

RewardU does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers operated by us. All data you create in the app — including habits, logged entries, and preferences — is stored on your device and, if you are signed in to iCloud, synced to your private iCloud account.

Data Storage & iCloud Sync

  • All habit and entry data is stored locally on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework.
  • If you are signed in to iCloud on your device, your data is automatically synced via Apple’s CloudKit to your personal, private iCloud storage. This allows you to access your habits and entries across all your Apple devices.
  • Your preferences (such as currency selection) are also synced via iCloud.
  • iCloud data is encrypted by Apple in transit and at rest. We have no access to your iCloud data.
  • You can disable iCloud sync for RewardU at any time in your device’s Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Apps Using iCloud.
  • No data is sent to any server operated by us or by third parties.

Third-Party Services

RewardU does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting services. The only external service used is Apple’s iCloud/CloudKit for optional data synchronization.

Data Sharing

RewardU does not share any data with third parties. Your data remains on your device and in your private iCloud account (if enabled). We have no access to it.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Delete individual entries by swiping left on them in the app.
  • Delete entire habits (including all associated entries) by swiping left on them in the habit list.
  • Deletions are synced across all your devices via iCloud.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. To also remove data from iCloud, disable iCloud sync for RewardU before uninstalling, or delete the data in Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.

Children’s Privacy

RewardU does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users. We do not operate any servers and have no access to user data.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
