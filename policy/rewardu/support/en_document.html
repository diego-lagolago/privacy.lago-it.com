Support — RewardU

App description, FAQ, and system requirements for RewardU.

App Description

RewardU — Reward yourself for your habits

RewardU makes it easy to build and maintain positive habits. Reward yourself for every completed unit — whether sports, reading, learning, or other activities. Track your progress, collect rewards, and redeem them for things that bring you joy.

Features

  • Create habits with flexible reward models (time-based or unit-based)
  • Earn monetary rewards or non-cash rewards
  • Goal-based habits with progress tracking
  • Home screen widgets (single and multi view)
  • iCloud synchronization across your devices
  • Fully available in German and English

FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

How does RewardU work?

You create habits and define how much reward you earn per unit (for example, per 30 minutes or per item). Every time you complete a unit, your reward is credited.

Are my data sent to a server?

No. All data are stored exclusively on your device. If iCloud is enabled, your data are additionally synchronized across your Apple devices via your personal iCloud account. There is no tracking and no data are shared with third parties.

Can I change my currency?

Yes. In settings, you can choose your preferred currency. All amounts are automatically displayed in the corresponding format.

What happens if I delete the app?

If iCloud sync is enabled, your data remain in your iCloud account and are restored after reinstallation. Without iCloud, data are lost when the app is deleted.

Can I use home screen widgets?

Yes. RewardU offers two widget types: a single widget for one specific habit and a multi widget that shows up to four habits at once. Both support configurable time ranges.

How can I contact the developer?

Send an email to support@lago-it.com — we are happy to help.

Supported Devices & System Requirements

  • Operating system: iOS 17.0 or newer
  • Devices: iPhone (all models supporting iOS 17)
  • Languages: German, English
  • Storage: iCloud synchronization optionally available
  • Widgets: Home screen widgets in small and medium sizes
